Please install the latest version of Java

Open "iamthegamerunme" to play

##########################################################

Controls: 

AWSD to move around
E to interact

##########################################################

Goal: 

get to the gate to the next level

##########################################################

Mechanics: 

1. pick up stone and put it on the column to open door 
   hint: walk to stone press e to pick up
         walk to column and press e to put stone on column
         press e again to pick up stone from column

2. start by going through blue portal 
   end by getting to orange portal
